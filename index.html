SuperApp — The Banking Pillar

Banking: The Pillar That Makes eToro a Daily App

Banking transforms eToro from a weekly trading platform into the financial SuperApp — one place that runs your entire financial life. Built on the same App Store, AI, and open API model as eToro Trading.

📄 Strategy Deck v1.1 🔒 Confidential — CEO Eyes 📅 March 2026

This document lays out the full 2026–2028 strategy, competitive analysis, product roadmap, team structure, partnership decisions, and go-to-market approach for transforming eToro Money into a banking SuperApp — with the App Store, AI agents, and eToro's open API as both the delivery engine and the client-facing platform — fully aligned with eToro Trading's approach.

00EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Opportunity

eToro has 40 million users who trust us with their money — and then open another app to pay rent, split dinner, or check their balance. That second app is the opportunity.

Banking isn't a side feature. It's the foundation that turns eToro from a weekly investing app into a daily financial app. Revolut, Robinhood, and N26 are all racing toward the same thesis: win the whole financial life. We have something they don't — a social investing platform, copy trading, and a community that's hard to replicate. They're adding investing to banking. We're adding banking to investing. Different product, different moat.

And we're building it on the same platform rails as Trading — the App Store and eToro's open API. Clients and developers won't just use banking features. They'll build new ones — automated savings rules, spending bots, custom allocation agents — using AI and our API. The same ecosystem model, applied to banking.

And we're not starting from zero:

1M active users $3B deposits 130K cards 2 EMI licences

The rails are live. What's missing is the full experience.

The vision: one app where you earn, save, spend, and invest — and they're all connected. Salary arrives, Smart Sweep allocates it. Buy coffee, Stock-Back puts £0.14 into Apple shares. Copy a top investor, funded from your savings buffer. No switching apps. One financial loop.

If we get this right, banking becomes the #1 acquisition channel, user frequency shifts from ~1×/week to 1–3×/day, cards activate across all geos, we unlock meaningful revenue diversification, and eToro becomes the financial app for the whole family.

The plan — three tracks in parallel:

Close the Gap
Virtual cards, Apple Pay, direct debits, multi-currency. Table stakes, shipped fast.
Only eToro
Goal Investor, NCW Debit Card, Smart Sweep, AI Auto-Allocation. Features that leverage eToro's unique assets.
Expand
Joint accounts, international transfers, credit, insurance. The moat.

The accelerator: most of these features won't be built the traditional way — and this is where banking fully aligns with eToro's company-wide strategy. The App Store is the primary vehicle: new banking capabilities are built as standalone apps and integrated into the SuperApp. But the App Store isn't just our delivery mechanism. It's a platform that clients, developers, and partners use to build banking apps themselves — automated savings tools, spending analytics, payment automations — using eToro's open API and AI agents. This is the same model eToro Trading is adopting: open the API, let AI-powered agents build on top of it, let the ecosystem scale what no single team could. The Banking Apps Squad ships the first wave. The ecosystem builds the rest. To drive this, eToro Money will stand up a dedicated Banking Apps Squad — a senior technical lead (architecture, eToro API integration, security), a junior developer, a product owner (design, user flows, feature specs), and a business owner (commercial logic, compliance, stakeholder coordination) — focused solely on shipping banking apps to the Store.

Combined with AI-assisted development across the stack — from code generation to testing to compliance review — we're targeting 10× delivery velocity from H2 2026. This is already how eToro Trading builds. Banking will use the same tools, the same AI agents, and the same development workflow.

We're also exploring a fintech acquisition to accelerate time to market. Two partner decisions — Tribe and banking infrastructure — need resolution in Q2 and are detailed in this document. Delivering on this plan will require a clear resource allocation decision — dedicated engineering capacity, a senior banking PM hire, and leadership commitment to treating banking as a top-priority program alongside the App Store and AI initiatives.

The foundation is built. This is the plan to get banking right.

4 Product Roadmap & Execution Based on AI/App Store

25 Features, 3 Tracks

Three parallel tracks across five phases. Track 1 closes the gap with competitors. Track 2 builds what only eToro can. Track 3 is the next wave. Timelines will be set once resources are committed.

Our Approach: Two Directions at Once

This roadmap works on two levels simultaneously:

1. Close the gap. Virtual cards, multi-currency, spending tools, Apple Pay — the table stakes that Revolut, N26, and Wise already have. Without these, we don't have permission to compete. Track 1 is about earning the right to be in the conversation.

2. Leverage our leadership in trading and crypto. Round-up investing, Smart Sweep, NCW Debit Card, stock gifting, AI auto-allocation — features that only eToro can build because we already have the trading engine, the social network, and the crypto infrastructure. No neo-bank has this. They're trying to bolt on trading. We're building banking on top of trading.

The competitors are going from banking → trading. We're going from trading → banking. That's a fundamentally different starting position — and a faster path to the SuperApp.

How we deliver: eToro's upcoming App Store is the primary vehicle for launching new capabilities. Each feature follows a two-step model: build as an app → integrate into the SuperApp. Combined with AI-assisted development, we are counting on 10× delivery velocity from H2 2026 and beyond to meet the ambitious scope of this roadmap.

For the App Store to work for banking, eToro Money must build a robust Money API — exposing account balances, transaction data, card controls, and payment triggers with the same quality and documentation as eToro Trading's API. This is what lets external developers and AI agents build banking apps. Without it, the App Store is closed to banking. This API is a prerequisite, not a phase-two item.

The roadmap naturally splits into two layers:

INFRA Platform rails built by eToro core — card issuance, IBAN, multi-currency ledger, Direct Debit, Apple Pay. These require deep integration and can't be decoupled into standalone apps.

APP Features built for the App Store — savings pockets, round-ups, budgeting, gifting, P2P. These plug into the infrastructure layer and ship as independent apps first.

The Team: eToro Money will stand up a dedicated Banking Apps Squad — a small, fast-moving unit designed to ship App Store features at startup speed. The squad: one senior technical lead (full-stack, owns architecture, eToro API integration, and security), one junior developer (builds alongside, ramps fast), a product owner (owns design, user flows, and feature specs), and a business owner (owns commercial logic, compliance alignment, and stakeholder coordination). This squad's sole mission: take features from this roadmap, build them as apps, and get them live in the App Store.

TRACK 1
Close the Gap

Virtual Card + Apple Pay (WIP) INFRA
Every single competitor has this. It's the #1 reason users don't use the eToro card daily. Without mobile wallet support, the card is stuck in the physical world — no online checkout, no tap-to-pay. This is table stakes, and it's our biggest gap.

Card for Rest of World — USD-Based Global Card (WIP) INFRA
A dollar-based card offered to club members worldwide, powered by Visa's cross-border network with existing licences and banking partners. Multi-Currency Balances (WIP) INFRA
Revolut offers 36+ currencies. Wise offers 40+. eToro users trade in USD but live in GBP/EUR. Without multi-currency balances, every transaction involves an FX conversion — and users feel it. This is core to being a global financial app.
4
Card Connected to Multi-Currency Balances INFRA
Once you have multi-currency balances, the card must spend from them. Travel to Spain, spend EUR. Fly to the US, spend USD. No markup, no conversion surprise. Revolut and Wise built their brand on this — it's expected.
5
Card / Apple Pay Deposit to IBAN INFRA
Users want to top up their account instantly — tap a card or Apple Pay and money is there. Right now, IBAN funding via bank transfer is slow. Instant deposits eliminate friction and make the account feel like a real bank account.
6
Savings Pockets (with Interest) APP
Revolut Vaults, N26 Spaces, Wise Jars — every neo-bank lets you separate money into goals. Basic savings pockets with interest are table stakes. This is what gets us permission to play in the savings space.
7
Spending Analytics + Instant Notifications APP
This is the daily engagement engine. Instant push on every transaction, monthly spending breakdowns, category tagging. It's what makes a neo-bank feel alive. Without it, the app is silent between trades — and users forget to come back.
8
Direct Debit Outbound (EU) INFRA
UK already has this — it's live. But EU users can't pay bills via direct debit yet. If you want to be someone's primary bank, you need to pay their bills. Direct debits for rent, utilities, subscriptions — this is what locks users into the platform.
9
Geo Expansion — UAE + Singapore INFRA
High-growth, high-wealth markets where eToro already has active users but no banking product. UAE and Singapore are fintech-friendly regulatory environments with strong demand for multi-currency and investment-linked banking.
10
Interest on Cash APP
Revolut pays 3-4%. Robinhood pays 4%. Trade Republic pays 3%. Users now expect their idle cash to earn. eToro can route this through the investment entity or a partner bank — it doesn't require the EMI licence, but it requires a solution.
11
Family Balances & Cards APP
Family accounts and kids cards bring the next generation into eToro. Revolut and Robinhood both offer this. Parents manage sub-accounts, set spending limits, and introduce their children to saving and investing — all within one app.

Round-Up Investing APP
Buy coffee for £3.40, round up £0.60 into your portfolio. This is the ultimate investing↔banking bridge — it turns every card swipe into a micro-investment. Revolut has it, Robinhood has it. Only eToro can combine it with CopyTrader and SmartPortfolios.

Smart Sweep APP
Idle cash sitting in the banking account auto-allocates into a SmartPortfolio when it passes a threshold. The user sets it once — "keep £500 liquid, invest the rest." Money works while they sleep. This is what makes eToro a financial brain, not just a bank.

Spend crypto directly via your eToro card. Architecture: NCW → IBAN → Card — the card only sees fiat, keeping the EMI clean. No competitor combines a non-custodial wallet with a Visa debit card in one app. This is a crypto-native banking feature.
4
Stock Gifting APP
Robinhood just launched this at their "Take Flight" event. Gift a friend $50 of Apple stock instead of a gift card. Social by design — fits eToro's DNA perfectly. Combine with the social feed and it becomes viral. eToro already has social + trading; adding gifting + banking connects them.

AI Auto-Allocation APP
An AI agent that looks at your income, spending patterns, goals, and risk profile — then automatically splits your money between spending, saving, and investing. Because it's built on eToro's open API, advanced users and developers can create their own allocation agents — custom rules, custom triggers, their logic running on our infrastructure. Same model as Trading.
6
Goal Investor — Savings That Invest APP
Savings pockets that don't just sit there — they invest toward your goal. Set a target ("Tokyo Trip — £3,000"), and Goal Investor builds a risk-adjusted portfolio to get you there. No neo-bank offers this. Revolut Vaults hold cash. eToro Goal Investor grows it. This is the investing↔banking bridge that only we can build.

What This Plan Requires

1
AI-Assisted Development
AI-assisted development serves two practical roles. Internally: code generation, testing, and compliance review across the stack — the same toolchain eToro Trading uses. Externally: eToro's API and AI agent framework let developers build banking apps for the App Store. Both roles depend on a well-built Money API (see below).
2
eToro Money API
The App Store model requires banking data and actions to be accessible via API — account balances, transaction history, card controls, payment initiation. This needs the same investment in documentation and developer experience as eToro Trading's API. It's the foundation that makes everything else in this plan scalable.
4
Strengthen the Banking Team
The existing banking team needs to be strengthened significantly. One option is reallocating the Denmark-based engineering team — they have the skills and capacity. If not Denmark, we need to invest in additional headcount or partner resources to close the gap.
4
Senior PM from a Competitor — Must Hire
eToro does not have experienced banking product management for a plan this ambitious. We need to hire a senior PM who has built this before — from Revolut, N26, Wise, or Monzo. Someone who knows the playbook, the pitfalls, and the pace required.
5
Tribe Bottleneck — Acknowledged Risk
Tribe controls the ledger and card processing. Their delivery velocity directly constrains ours. This plan assumes Tribe delivers — but the partnership decision (stay, acquire, or replace) will define whether this timeline is achievable.
6
JPM/Citi/HSBC — Scope & Resources Unknown
The banking partner integration could range from lightweight API work to a full IBAN migration. Until the big-picture conversation happens, we can't size the engineering effort or timeline impact. This is a known unknown.
7
Acquire a Small Company — Accelerate Time to Market
Acquiring a small, mature fintech company brings both a proven product and experienced talent (acqui-hire) — dramatically shortening time to market. A search process is already underway and should be continued with urgency. The right acquisition could leapfrog months of in-house development.

6 Strategic Partnerships

Two Important Decisions

The banking strategy depends on two critical partnerships. Both carry major opportunity and major risk. These are the decisions that define the pace and shape of everything we build.

DECISION 1
Banking Partner — JP Morgan, Citi or HSBC
📍 Where We Are Today
JPM is already a live partner — they hold client accounts and corporate funds. Direct debit infrastructure discussions are ongoing. But the big picture conversation — "become the core banking partner powering eToro's 40M users" — has never happened. That conversation needs to happen.
✅ The Opportunity
Solves our hardest gaps. Interest on cash, deposit protection, multi-currency accounts (Virtual Account Management), international transfers — all things outside EMI scope but within JPM's banking infrastructure.
EMI handles 80%. JPM handles the other 20%. 80% of our roadmap is buildable under EMI. The remaining ones — interest, deposit insurance, institutional-grade FX — are exactly what a tier-1 bank provides.
"JPM Inside" branding. No neo-bank can claim this. Revolut, N26, Monzo are their own banks. eToro would be backed by a top-3 global bank. Strong trust signal for large depositors, differentiates the competitive positioning.
Win-win deal structure. JPM gets access to 40M+ users without building consumer apps. eToro gets institutional banking without a 5-year regulatory build. Both sides have clear value.
Crypto bridge potential. JPM's Kinexys (JPM Coin) could enable tokenized deposit settlement — a future path for NCW-to-card and crypto-to-fiat flows.
⚠️ The Risk
Dependency on a giant. JPM's priorities are not eToro's priorities. Integration timelines could be measured in years, not months. A tier-1 bank moves at tier-1 speed.
Commercial terms unknown. Revenue share, per-transaction fees, exclusivity — none of this has been discussed. The economics could be favourable or could eat into margins.
Regulatory complexity. JPM partnership may require regulatory approvals on both sides. Compliance alignment across jurisdictions adds time.
Alternatives: Citi, HSBC. Both offer similar infrastructure and may be more agile with fintech partnerships. Having backup options keeps leverage in the negotiation.
🔀 Possible Directions
A
Full Strategic Partnership
JPM becomes the core banking partner — deposits, FX, payments, deposit insurance, "JPM Inside" branding. Deep integration, long-term commitment, maximum upside.
B
Targeted Integration
Use JPM for specific gaps only — interest on cash (JPM holds deposits), multi-currency (Virtual Accounts), direct debits. Keep the rest independent. Lower risk, faster execution.
C
Multi-Track — JPM / Citi / HSBC
Explore multiple partners in parallel. Create competitive tension. Choose the partner that moves fastest and offers the best terms. Avoids single-point dependency.
DECISION 2
Tribe — Stay, Acquire, or Replace
📍 Where We Are Today
Tribe is eToro Money's card processor and core ledger. They handle authorization, settlement, card issuance, and the transaction ledger. They are effectively a team extension for the Money BU. The relationship works — but Tribe controls the ledger, which means Tribe controls the pace. And the pace has been slow.
✅ What Tribe Gives Us
Proven infrastructure. Cards work. Payments settle. The system is live and stable across UK and EU. Replacing it is non-trivial.
Speed to market for new geos. Tribe has partnerships for Australia and potentially other markets. Going through Tribe for geo expansion could be faster than building from scratch.
Team extension. The Money BU is small. Tribe provides operational capacity that would be expensive to build in-house.
⚠️ The Risk
They own the ledger. Every new feature, every change, every integration goes through Tribe. If they're slow, we're slow. If they de-prioritize us, we're stuck. This is the single biggest infrastructure risk in the banking plan.
Delivery velocity has been low. Features that should take weeks take months. The gap between what we want to build and what Tribe can deliver is growing.
Strategic misalignment. eToro wants to become a SuperApp. Tribe is a card processor. Their roadmap may not align with eToro's ambition — especially for features like round-ups, Smart Sweep, or NCW integration that sit outside traditional card processing.
Lock-in. Migrating off Tribe would be a major project — account migration, card re-issuance, ledger migration. The longer we stay, the harder it gets to leave.
🔀 Possible Directions
A
Stay + Negotiate
Keep Tribe but renegotiate SLAs, delivery commitments, and priority. Push for dedicated engineering resources. Accept the dependency but manage it aggressively. Lowest disruption, but doesn't solve the root problem.
B
Acquire Tribe (or Key Team)
Buy the team and technology. Bring the ledger in-house. Full control over roadmap and velocity. Expensive upfront, but eliminates the dependency permanently and gives eToro a proprietary payments stack.
C
Migrate to a New Processor
Replace Tribe with a modern card-as-a-service provider (Marqeta, Adyen, Stripe Issuing). Painful migration — card re-issuance, ledger rebuild — but opens the door to better velocity and API-first integration for features like round-ups and NCW.
D
Build In-House (Gradually)
Start building a parallel ledger and processing layer. Run Tribe for existing products while new features (round-ups, Smart Sweep, NCW card) run on the new stack. Migrate over time. Requires significant engineering investment but gives full independence.

Compliance Guardrails

Safeguarding: All customer funds held in segregated safeguarding accounts at ClearBank (UK) and Banking Circle (EU). This is non-negotiable under EMI regulation.
Settlement: Prefunded scheme settlement accounts for card transactions. eToro Money does not take credit risk on card spend.
Crypto: NCW Debit Card architecture keeps the EMI clean — NCW converts to fiat at the IBAN level; the card only sees fiat. Crypto trading and custody handled by separate eToro entities (FCA crypto registration in UK, CySEC in EU).
Escalation rule: Any new feature that changes safeguarding mechanics, creates financial exposure, introduces new asset classes, alters fund flow architecture, or targets a new country — must go through compliance review before development begins.
